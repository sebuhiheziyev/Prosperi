Privacy Policy

Prosperi.io – a product of Scorpios Tech

The following Privacy Policy applies to Prosperi.io (hereinafter referred to as the “website” and/or “product”), more precisely to the description of the information processing for support of our product.

While working on our product, our primary intent is to create an effective tool that will improve the lives of our users. However, we have not overlooked the issue of protecting the privacy of everyone using our website. We take your privacy very seriously, and we will share what data we collect, and how we use it to make this process as transparent as possible.

This privacy policy is compliant with the provisions of the EU’s General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and meets safety standards applicable in the USA, while for the purposes of this policy, the term of “personal data” used was taken from the provisions of the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

What Information We Collect:

  • Information provided by the user

As a part of the onboarding while answering the questions and entering personal data (gender, age, educational degree, email address, financial status, etc. (please, see the list of questions in the Questionnaire section)) on the website for further construction of the personalized package, the user must read and accept this Privacy Policy and Terms and Conditions (according to the Article 6 (1) (a) of the GDPR). Whereupon the information is transmitted to us and stored in our database with legitimate interests and compliance with legal obligations.

The user has the right to correct or remove his/her personal data by contacting our Support Service support@prosperi.io

(!) Our product, unlike most others, does NOT use cookies and other similar technologies to provide services.

Why We Collect Personal Data

Going through the onboarding process, the user provides us with his/her personal data for:

  • Construction of the personalized plan to boost user’s financial situation depending on the Subscription chosen;

  • Providing the user with an individual approach (quality assurance, task preparation, etc.)

The product only requests information/data relevant to its core functionality, and only collects and uses information/data that is required to accomplish the relevant task(s).
